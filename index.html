⏱️ Processing Timeline (50 min video)

Stage 1: Video Preprocessing
5-10 minutes
Extract frames, synchronize videos, quality checks
Stage 2: Camera Calibration
15-30 minutes
COLMAP feature detection, matching, SfM reconstruction
Stage 3: NeRF Training
2-4 hours
Neural radiance field training with Instant-NGP
Stage 4: Gaussian Splatting
30-60 minutes
Optimize for real-time rendering (60+ FPS)
Stage 5: Human Detection
10-20 minutes
YOLOv8 detection + DeepSORT tracking
Stage 6: Pose Estimation
15-30 minutes
MediaPipe 33-point pose estimation
Stage 7: Avatar Generation
20-40 minutes
SMPL-X parametric body model with textures
Stage 8: Voice Cloning
10-20 minutes
Extract audio, clone voices, train TTS
Stage 9: AI Training
5-10 minutes
Build conversational AI with GPT-4/Claude
Stage 10: Dataset Export
10-20 minutes
Package and export USD, ROS, URDF formats
Total: 4-7 hours
Complete VR world with interactive AI characters
