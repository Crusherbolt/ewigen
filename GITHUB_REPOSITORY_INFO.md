### **Version 2: Technical/Academic Focus**

```markdown
# Ewigen - Multi-View 3D Reconstruction for Robotics

Research platform for generating high-quality synthetic training datasets from real-world video captures. Combines state-of-the-art computer vision and deep learning techniques.

## 🔬 Technical Approach

**Scene Reconstruction Pipeline**
1. Multi-view video synchronization and preprocessing
2. Structure from Motion (SfM) with COLMAP
3. Neural Radiance Fields (Instant-NGP) training
4. Gaussian Splatting optimization for real-time rendering

**Human Analysis Pipeline**
1. Multi-person detection and tracking across views
2. 3D pose estimation and temporal smoothing
3. Parametric body model fitting (SMPL-X)
4. Motion retargeting and animation export

**Dataset Generation**
1. Semantic segmentation and object detection
2. Depth map and normal map generation
3. Multi-modal annotation (bounding boxes, keypoints, masks)
4. Format conversion for robotics frameworks

## 📈 Performance

- Processing: 4-7 hours for 50 minutes of video
- Output: 10-25 GB per scene
- Accuracy: Sub-centimeter 3D reconstruction
- Scale: Supports 100+ hours of video per project

## 🎓 Research Applications

- Embodied AI training
- Human-robot interaction datasets
- Scene understanding benchmarks
- Sim-to-real transfer learning
- Multi-modal perception research

## 🔧 Built With

PyTorch, COLMAP, Instant-NGP, YOLOv8, MediaPipe, SMPL-X, FastAPI, Celery
```
